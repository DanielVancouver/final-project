ABOUT ME

In 2020, feeling constrained by my previous life, I made the bold decision to leave my job after nine years, alongside my girlfriend. We set off on an adventure to explore the world, traveling extensively across Europe, where I embraced van life with my dog and cat. This journey allowed us to experience beautiful cities and breathtaking landscapes, enriching our lives with unforgettable moments. After our time in Europe, we ventured to Bali and Australia, countries that I've loved. Living on the road brought a sense of freedom and connection to nature that I had been craving. Now, I’m in Vancouver, diving into new experiences and continuing my journey of discovery. Each day offers new opportunities, and I’m excited to see where this path leads me next!
